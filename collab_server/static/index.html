How to Get Access

1

Register in the app

Download a preview build or contact us directly. Open the app and fill in your email, name, and institution.

2

Wait for approval

Your request is reviewed by the lab. This usually takes 1–2 business days.

3

Receive your confirmation email

Once approved, you'll get an email confirming your account. Log in with the email and password you registered with.

4

Run Quick Analysis

Drop in a .mov, .mp4, or .avi brightfield recording and run the full pipeline — segmentation, tracking, beat metrics, contractility, Excel export.
