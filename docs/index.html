Quick Start

1

Download & Extract

Download the zip for your platform and extract it anywhere.

2

Run the app

Windows: double-click MyoOptix.exe  ·  Mac: open MyoOptix.app
First launch downloads model weights (~93 MB, one-time).

3

Open a project or Quick Analysis

Drag in a video, choose segmentation method, click Run.

4

Export to Excel

Review results per ROI, then export beat metrics and raw data.
